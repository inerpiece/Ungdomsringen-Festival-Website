Who are we?

Ungdomsringens Musikfestival is DK's largest festival for leisure youth clubs and youth school bands. The purpose of the Youth Ring Music Festival is to give participants the opportunity to go on a fun and adventurous weekend trip to Aalborg with the music in the center. And everyone is welcome! We hope you fill the bus and take the whole club or junior school team to Aalborg. There is free entrance for everyone.
